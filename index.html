Mouse Estrous Cycle Visualization

Exploring temperature and activity patterns across different phases of the estrous cycle in female mice

Estrous Cycle Phases:

  • Proestrus: Follicular development; rising estrogen; increased activity begins
  • Estrus: Ovulation; peak sexual receptivity and highest acti
  • Metestrus: Transition; estrogen drops
  • Diestrus: Low activity; uterine lining maintained

How to Use This Tool:

  1. Select an exploration method from the dropdown menu
  2. Choose specific mice to include in your analysis
  3. Select which days (and corresponding phases) to display in each line
  4. Use the time slider to explore temperature and activity at specific times of day
  5. Toggle between raw values and Z-score normalized views for better comparison
